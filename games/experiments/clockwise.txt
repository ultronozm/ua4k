;; CLOCKWISE — an indirect-control herding puzzle.
;;
;; A wind-up robot marches one step forward every turn. When it cannot
;; step forward, it spends the turn rotating 90 degrees clockwise.
;; You can never touch it: you steer it by standing in its way, pushing
;; crates into its path, and filling pits. Dock it on 'g' to win.
;; If it walks into a pit, it is wrecked.
